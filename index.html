What is BiguaSim?

Due to the intricate tasks and environments, particular complexities arise in developing and testing algorithms for autonomous heterogeneous vehicle interactions. Additionally, there is a need for a large amount of high-fidelity annotated data in various conditions and environments. Focused on these needs, we introduce BiguaSim as a novel open-source multi-domain simulator built on Unreal Engine 5 (UE5). BiguaSim aims to generate synthetic, realistic, annotated data based on various sensor implementations, for both air and water vehicles. Our simulator includes multi-agent support with GPU-enabled dynamics and integration with robotics tools like ROS2 and ArduPilot. To demonstrate the simulator, we conducted a multi-domain data collection employing a sample coverage strategy.

Architecture

BiguaSim’s architecture is modular, supporting domain-specific simulation layers for air and underwater vehicles. The simulator integrates with robotics middleware such as ROS2 and ArduPilot, enabling realistic control and sensor feedback loops in complex environments.

Core Features

  • Aerial Domain: Realistic UAV dynamics with wind simulation, LiDAR, and RGB cameras.
  • Underwater Domain: AUVs with sonar, DVL, and hydrodynamic models for realistic motion.
  • Hybrid Scenarios: Coordinated operations between air and underwater vehicles.
  • Dataset Generation: Synchronized multi-sensor data for AI and robotics research.

Sensor Outputs & Visual Results

BiguaSim provides a unified framework to visualize and export sensor data from multiple domains. The simulator supports camera feeds, LiDAR point clouds, sonar images, and depth maps.

Future Directions

BiguaSim continues to evolve as an open research platform for robotics simulation. Upcoming developments include advanced environmental modeling, multi-robot coordination, improved underwater acoustics, and reinforcement learning integration.
